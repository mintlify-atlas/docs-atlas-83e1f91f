## Deleting your organization

<Warning>
Deleting your organization is permanent and cannot be undone. All data will be lost.
</Warning>

To delete your organization, contact [help@incident.io](mailto:help@incident.io). 

### Requirements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify permissions">
    You must be the organization owner or have appropriate admin permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact from registered email">
    Contact us from your registered account email address or Slack
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide organization URL">
    Include your organization URL (e.g., `https://app.incident.io/your-org-name`)
  </Step>
</Steps>

The support team will handle organization deletion and plan cancellation.

### What happens after deletion

After deletion completes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Access removed" icon="lock">
    All users lose access to the organization
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications stop" icon="bell-slash">
    Automated emails and notifications stop
  </Card>
  <Card title="Confirmation sent" icon="envelope">
    You'll receive confirmation from support
  </Card>
  <Card title="Can recreate" icon="rotate">
    You can create a new account if you want to use incident.io again in the future
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Deleting your user account

Individual user accounts are synchronized with your Slack profile and cannot be deleted directly from incident.io.
