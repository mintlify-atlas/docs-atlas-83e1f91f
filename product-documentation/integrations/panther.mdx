# Adding Panther as an alert source

This article provides step by step instructions for setting up Panther as an alert source within incident.io.

## Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Alerts">
    Head over to the [Alerts](https://app.incident.io/~/alerts/sources) section in your incident.io dashboard

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-01.png" alt="Alerts section in incident.io dashboard" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the sources tab">
    Select the sources tab at the top of the page

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-02.png" alt="Sources tab at top of page" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create new alert source">
    Press the 'New alert source' button

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-03.png" alt="New alert source button" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Panther">
    Search for 'Panther' and click continue to create the alert source

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-04.png" alt="Search for Panther and click continue" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Panther Alert Destinations">
    Switch over to your Panther dashboard, and select 'Alert Destinations' under the 'Configure' section

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-05.png" alt="Alert Destinations in Panther dashboard" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create new destination">
    If you already have another alert destination set up, click the 'Create New' button in the top right hand corner. Otherwise, click 'Add your first Destination'.

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-06.png" alt="Create New button" />

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-07.png" alt="Add your first Destination button" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Custom Webhook">
    Choose 'Custom Webhook' from the list of destinations

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-08.png" alt="Choose Custom Webhook from destinations list" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure webhook">
    Copy the Webhook URL provided to you after you created a Panther alert source in the incident.io dashboard, and make sure to add a custom HTTP header for 'Authorization'. The value for this header is provided to you in the incident.io dashboard, alongside the Webhook URL.

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-09.png" alt="Copy Webhook URL and add Authorization header" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test the integration">
    Click 'Add Destination' and then 'Send Test Alert' to test your integration. You should be able to see a test alert in your incident.io dashboard!

    <img src="/images/integrations/panther/panther-10.png" alt="Add Destination and Send Test Alert" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
