# OpsLevel

Connect your OpsLevel service catalog to incident.io to automatically import your services, teams, and metadata into the incident.io Catalog.

## Setting Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Integrations">
    Navigate to [Settings > Integrations](https://app.incident.io/settings/integrations)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Press 'Install' next to OpsLevel">
    Find OpsLevel in the list of available integrations and click the Install button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fetch your API key from OpsLevel">
    You'll need to create an API key by heading to the settings page of [your OpsLevel account](https://app.opslevel.com/api_tokens).

    <Note>
    Make sure the API key has the necessary permissions to read catalog data from OpsLevel.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your API key and press 'Save'">
    Paste the API key into the integration configuration form and save your settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your connected Catalog entities">
    Go to [Catalog](https://app.incident.io/catalog) to view and manage the entities imported from OpsLevel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Synced

Once connected, incident.io will automatically import and sync:

- **Services**: All services defined in your OpsLevel catalog
- **Teams**: Team ownership information
- **Metadata**: Service metadata including descriptions, lifecycle, tier, and custom properties
- **Dependencies**: Service relationships and dependencies
- **Tags**: All tags and labels associated with your services

<Info>
The sync happens automatically on a regular basis to keep your incident.io Catalog up to date with changes in OpsLevel.
</Info>

## Benefits

- **Automatic Service Discovery**: No need to manually maintain your service catalog in incident.io
- **Accurate Ownership**: Always know who owns which service during an incident
- **Rich Context**: Access all your service metadata when responding to incidents
- **Single Source of Truth**: Keep OpsLevel as your primary service catalog while leveraging incident.io for incident management
- **Maturity Tracking**: Leverage OpsLevel's service maturity data during incident response
