## Overview

Our AI-powered features leverage machine learning to enhance your incident management workflow. From automatic summarization to intelligent follow-up suggestions, AI helps your team work smarter during critical incidents.

## Getting Started with AI Features

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable AI features">
    Check your organization settings to enable the AI features you want to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try suggested summaries">
    During your next incident, use the suggested summary feature to quickly document what happened.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Scribe">
    Configure Scribe to automatically join and transcribe your incident calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review suggestions">
    Pay attention to AI suggestions during incidents and refine based on what's helpful for your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
AI features learn and improve over time. The more you use them, the more tailored they become to your organization's incident patterns.
</Tip>
