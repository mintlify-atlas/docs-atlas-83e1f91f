## How AI enhances incident response

<Steps>
  <Step title="During incidents">
    AI suggests summaries and follow-ups in real-time, helping responders stay focused on resolution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="In incident calls">
    Scribe automatically transcribes and summarizes calls, ensuring no important details are lost.
  </Step>
  <Step title="After incidents">
    AI-generated content helps with documentation, retrospectives, and learning from past incidents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continuous improvement">
    AI learns from your incident patterns to provide increasingly relevant suggestions over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
All AI features are designed to augment your incident response process, not replace human judgment. You always maintain full control over what content is used and when.
</Note>

<Tip>
AI features work best when you provide rich incident context through detailed updates, clear communication, and comprehensive incident data.
</Tip>
