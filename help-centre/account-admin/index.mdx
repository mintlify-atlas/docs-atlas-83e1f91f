## Overview

As an account administrator, you have access to organization-wide settings that control how your team uses incident.io. From managing user permissions to configuring billing, this section covers everything you need to administer your account.

## Common Administrative Tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up user permissions">
    Configure roles and permissions to control who can perform different actions in incident.io.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure billing">
    Set up your payment method and choose the right plan for your organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable security features">
    Implement SAML SSO and other security measures to protect your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor usage">
    Use audit logs to track activity and ensure compliance with your organization's policies.
  </Step>
</Steps>
