## Common issues and solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Zoom: Diagnosing common issues" icon="video">
    **Common Zoom problems:**
    - Scribe not joining calls
    - Missing transcription
    - Audio quality issues
    - Permission errors
    - Zoom account configuration
    
    **Quick fixes:**
    - Verify Zoom integration is properly configured
    - Check that Scribe has permission to join meetings
    - Ensure the Zoom account has recording permissions
    - Verify network connectivity
    
    [View detailed Zoom troubleshooting →](/help-centre/scribe-and-incident-calls/zoom-diagnosing-common-issues)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Google Meet: Diagnosing common issues" icon="google">
    **Common Google Meet problems:**
    - Scribe cannot access the meeting
    - Recording not starting
    - Transcription missing or incomplete
    - Permission denied errors
    - Calendar integration issues
    
    **Quick fixes:**
    - Check Google Calendar permissions
    - Verify Google Meet recording settings
    - Ensure Scribe is added as a participant
    - Review Google Workspace policies
    
    [View detailed Google Meet troubleshooting →](/help-centre/scribe-and-incident-calls/google-meet-diagnosing-common-issues)
  </Accordion>

## How Scribe works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Call creation">
    When an incident call is started, Scribe automatically receives an invitation to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Joining the call">
    Scribe joins the call as a participant and begins recording and transcription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-time transcription">
    As the call progresses, Scribe transcribes the conversation in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI summarization">
    After the call ends, AI generates a summary of key points and action items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Documentation">
    Transcription and summary are automatically added to the incident timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Before the call" icon="clipboard-check">
    - Ensure integration is properly configured
    - Verify permissions are granted
    - Test with a non-incident call first
    - Check your organization's recording policies
  </Card>
  <Card title="During the call" icon="microphone">
    - Speak clearly for better transcription
    - Use names when addressing participants
    - Explicitly state action items
    - Pause briefly between topics
  </Card>
  <Card title="After the call" icon="list-check">
    - Review the AI-generated summary
    - Edit or add context as needed
    - Verify action items are captured
    - Share summary with stakeholders
  </Card>
  <Card title="Troubleshooting" icon="wrench">
    - Check platform-specific guides first
    - Verify all permissions are granted
    - Review integration settings
    - Contact support if issues persist
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
