## Overview

Incident calls are a critical part of coordinating your response to major incidents. Scribe automatically joins your calls to provide transcription and AI-powered summarization, ensuring nothing important gets lost in the heat of an incident.

## What is Scribe?

  Learn about Scribe's capabilities and how it enhances your incident calls.

<Info>
Scribe uses AI to transcribe and summarize your incident calls, making it easy to review what was discussed and capture action items automatically.
</Info>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Scribe isn't joining calls" icon="user-slash">
    If Scribe isn't automatically joining your incident calls, check:
    - Your integration settings for the video conferencing platform
    - That Scribe has the necessary permissions
    - Your organization's call automation settings
    
    See platform-specific guides for detailed troubleshooting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transcription quality issues" icon="message-slash">
    Transcription quality can be affected by:
    - Background noise or poor audio quality
    - Multiple people speaking at once
    - Strong accents or technical jargon
    
    Encourage clear speaking and minimize background noise for best results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Call creation failing" icon="phone-slash">
    If incident calls aren't being created:
    - Verify your video conferencing integration is connected
    - Check that you have the required permissions
    - Ensure your organization's call settings are configured correctly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
