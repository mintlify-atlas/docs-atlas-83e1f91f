## Overview

incident.io's reporting and insights features help you understand your incident patterns, measure response effectiveness, and identify areas for improvement. From customizable views to scheduled reports, you have the tools to turn incident data into actionable insights.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Explore your data">
    Start by using the built-in insights views to understand your incident trends, frequency, and response times.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create saved views">
    Build custom views that focus on the specific incidents, time periods, or metrics relevant to your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up scheduled reports">
    Configure automated reports to keep stakeholders informed about incident patterns and team performance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build custom dashboards">
    For advanced analysis, create custom dashboards that combine multiple metrics and visualizations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Regularly reviewing incident insights helps teams identify systemic issues and improve their incident response processes over time.
</Tip>
