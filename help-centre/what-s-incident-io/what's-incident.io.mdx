## What's incident.io?

Fair question!

We're a **modern incident management platform**. We help high-growth companies like yours declare, collaborate, communicate around and learn from events that disturb their normal course of business - from critical infrastructure being down, to data breaches and security incidents.

<Info>
We're already helping hundreds of **happy customers like Etsy, Miro, Hashicorp, Skyscanner, and StubHub** reduce their downtime and improve their incident response.
</Info>

## What Problems Do We Solve?

When an incident strikes, teams often struggle with critical questions:

  <Card title="Finding the Right People" icon="users">
    "Do we have the right people in the room to help?"
  </Card>
  <Card title="Learning from Incidents" icon="book">
    "Where's the post-mortem?"
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tracking Performance" icon="chart-line">
    "How many incidents did we have this month? How long did it take us to solve?"
  </Card>

incident.io provides answers to all of these questions and more, helping you respond faster and learn from every incident.

## Key Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Incident Declaration & Collaboration" icon="siren">
    Quickly declare incidents from Slack or the web dashboard, automatically create dedicated channels, and bring together the right people with smart routing and escalation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Real-time Communication" icon="comments">
    Keep everyone informed with automatic status updates, timeline tracking, and seamless integration with your communication tools.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="On-call Management" icon="calendar-clock">
    Build flexible on-call schedules, set up escalation paths, and ensure the right people are notified when incidents occur.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Status Pages" icon="signal">
    Communicate transparently with customers through public status pages, keeping them informed about incidents and maintenance windows.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Post-incident Learning" icon="lightbulb">
    Create comprehensive post-mortems, track follow-up actions, and build a knowledge base from past incidents.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Analytics & Insights" icon="chart-bar">
    Understand your incident patterns with detailed reporting, track team performance, and identify areas for improvement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
