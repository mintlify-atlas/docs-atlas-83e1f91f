## What's incident.io?

Fair question!

We're a **modern incident management platform**. We help high-growth companies like yours declare, collaborate, communicate around and learn from events that disturb their normal course of business - from critical infrastructure being down, to data breaches and security incidents.

## Key Questions We Help You Answer

We're already helping hundreds of [happy customers](https://incident.io/love) **like Etsy, Miro, Hashicorp, Skyscanner, and StubHub** reduce their downtime and answer questions like:

<Steps>
  <Step title="What's happening?">
    *"I just got pulled into an incident: what's happening, and how bad is it?"*
    
    Get instant context on any incident with clear severity levels, timelines, and status updates.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Right people in the room?">
    *"Do we have the right people in the room to help?"*
    
    Automatically pull in the right responders based on on-call schedules and escalation policies.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Where's the post-mortem?">
    *"Where's the post-mortem?"*
    
    Generate comprehensive post-mortems with automated timelines and follow-up actions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="How are we doing?">
    *"How many incidents did we have this month? How long did it take us to solve?"*
    
    Track metrics, trends, and insights to continuously improve your incident response.
  </Step>
</Steps>

We hope you'll be one of them!
