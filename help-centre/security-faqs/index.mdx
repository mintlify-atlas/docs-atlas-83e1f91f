## Authentication and access

<Steps>
  <Step title="User authentication">
    incident.io authenticates users through your communication platform (Slack or Microsoft Teams) or via SAML SSO.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permission management">
    Role-based access controls ensure users only have access to appropriate features and data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Session management">
    Secure session handling with automatic timeouts and refresh mechanisms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Multi-factor authentication">
    Leverage your identity provider's MFA capabilities for enhanced security.
  </Step>
</Steps>
