## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the installation">
    Begin by visiting the incident.io app installation page and clicking "Add to Slack" or "Add to Microsoft Teams".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    Review and approve the requested permissions. We only ask for what we need to provide you with the best incident management experience.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure your account">
    Once installed, you'll be guided through initial setup including creating your first incident workflow and configuring basic settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Add team members and set up on-call schedules to ensure the right people are notified during incidents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Need help?** If you encounter any issues during installation, check out our [troubleshooting guide](/help-centre/installing-incident-io/troubleshooting/index) or [contact our support team](/help-centre/help-feedback/contact-us).
</Note>
