## Troubleshooting

<Warning>
If you're experiencing issues with Microsoft Teams integration, start with these common troubleshooting guides.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check admin consent">
    Ensure your Teams admin has granted the necessary permissions through the admin consent flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify installation">
    Confirm that the incident.io app is properly installed and configured in your Teams workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review sign-in issues">
    Check our debugging guide if users are having trouble signing in during installation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues persist, reach out to our support team with details about your setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
