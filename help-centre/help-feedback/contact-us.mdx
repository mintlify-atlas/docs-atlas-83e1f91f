<Note>
Unlike other companies who optimize for self-serve customer service, we optimize for talking to as many of our users as possible.
</Note>

## Overview

We believe the best way to build a great product is by talking directly to the people who use it. That's why we make it easy to reach our support team whenever you need help.

## Contact Methods

### In-App Support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your organization selector">
    Click on your organization name in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Contact Support button">
    Look for the "Contact support" button in the organization selector menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Fill out the form with your question or issue, and our team will respond promptly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/help-centre/contact-us/screenshot-1.png" alt="Contact support button in organization selector" />
</Frame>

<Info>
If you're on one of our plans that includes a direct Slack or Microsoft Teams channel with our support team, you can reach us there for even faster responses.
</Info>

### Email Support

<Card title="Email our support team" icon="envelope" href="mailto:help@incident.io">
  Our support team is available at **help@incident.io**
</Card>

We monitor this inbox closely and aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.

## What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you faster, please include:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Describe the issue" icon="message">
    Provide a clear description of what you're trying to do and what's not working as expected.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Steps to reproduce" icon="list-ol">
    If reporting a bug, include the steps we can follow to see the same issue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Screenshots or recordings" icon="camera">
    Visual context helps us understand the issue more quickly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Organization details" icon="building">
    Let us know your organization name or the email domain you use to sign in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Response Times

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Direct Channel" icon="bolt">
    **Fastest response** - If you have a dedicated Slack/Teams channel with us, expect responses within minutes during business hours.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email & In-App" icon="clock">
    **Quick response** - We aim to respond to all email and in-app support requests within a few hours during business hours.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
For urgent production issues, make sure to indicate the urgency in your message so we can prioritize accordingly.
</Tip>

## Enterprise Support

If you're on an Enterprise plan, you have access to:

- **Dedicated support channel** - Direct Slack or Microsoft Teams channel with our team
- **Priority response times** - Faster SLAs for critical issues
- **Customer success manager** - A dedicated CSM to help you get the most out of incident.io
- **Regular check-ins** - Scheduled calls to discuss your needs and feedback

## Before Contacting Support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the docs">
    Search our documentation to see if your question is already answered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try troubleshooting guides">
    Look for troubleshooting articles related to your issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check system status">
    Visit our status page to see if there are any known issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still need help?">
    Contact us using one of the methods above - we're here to help!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## We Want to Hear From You

<Info>
Don't hesitate to reach out, even if you just have a question or want to share feedback. We genuinely want to talk to you!
</Info>

Whether you're reporting a bug, requesting a feature, or just have a question about how something works, we're here to help. Your feedback helps us build a better product for everyone.
