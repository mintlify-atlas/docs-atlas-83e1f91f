## In-app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open organization selector">
    Click on your organization name in the incident.io dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find 'Contact support'">
    Look for the "Contact support" button in the organization selector menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Describe your issue or question and submit the support request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a response">
    Our team will respond to you promptly through the same channel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/help-centre/contact-us/screenshot-1.png" alt="Contact support button in organization selector" />
</Frame>

<Note>
If you're on one of our plans that provides a direct Slack or Microsoft Teams channel with us, you can reach out directly through that channel for the fastest response.
</Note>

## Email

<Card title="Email our support team" icon="envelope">
  Our support team is available at **[help@incident.io](mailto:help@incident.io)**
</Card>

When emailing support, please include:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Your organization name" icon="building">
    Help us locate your account quickly by providing your organization name as it appears in incident.io.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Detailed description" icon="file-lines">
    Describe your issue or question in detail. The more context you provide, the faster we can help.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Steps to reproduce" icon="list-ol">
    If reporting a bug or issue, include the steps to reproduce the problem.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Screenshots or logs" icon="image">
    When relevant, attach screenshots or error logs to help us understand the issue.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Before contacting support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the documentation">
    Search our help center for articles related to your question. Many common issues have detailed guides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review recent changes">
    Check if any recent configuration changes might be related to your issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather information">
    Collect relevant details like error messages, screenshots, and steps to reproduce.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check status page">
    Visit our status page to see if there are any known service issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
