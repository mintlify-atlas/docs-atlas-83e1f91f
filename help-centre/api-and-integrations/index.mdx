## Popular Integrations

### Communication Platforms

Communication is critical during incidents. incident.io integrates deeply with your chat platform:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    Our Slack integration creates dedicated incident channels, sends notifications, and enables workflow automation directly from Slack.
    
    **Key capabilities:**
    - Automatic incident channel creation
    - Role and action notifications
    - Slash commands for quick actions
    - Status updates in channels
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Microsoft Teams">
    The Microsoft Teams integration provides similar functionality with Teams-native experiences.
    
    **Key capabilities:**
    - Incident team and channel management
    - Private incident support via chats
    - In-app tabs for incident details
    - Activity notifications
    
    [Learn more about Microsoft Teams](/help-centre/microsoft-teams/index)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your integrations">
    Identify which tools your team uses daily that should be connected to incident.io
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions">
    Understand what permissions each integration requires and coordinate with admins if needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure integrations">
    Navigate to Settings > Integrations in incident.io to connect your tools
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the integration">
    Verify the integration works by creating a test incident or triggering a test event
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document for your team">
    Share integration capabilities with your team so everyone knows how to leverage them
  </Step>
</Steps>
